Sore Throat or Strep Throat?

Are you feeling like you might have strep throat but wonder if it is just a sore throat? Answer a few short questions to find out!

Strep and a sore throat often present similar symptoms, but a sore throat is caused by a virus while strep is caused by a bacterial infection. Because of this it is very important to determine which one you have in order to properly recive treatment!
